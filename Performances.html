Performances

Lloyd Waller has performed extensively in a variety of loactions and with a variety of ensembles. He has perfomered most frequently with the Curb Youth Symphony while in high school, a prestigious youth orchestra located in Nashville, Tennessee, and more frequently now with the Frost Wind Ensemble as part of the Frost School of Music at the University of Miami. He was also lucky enough to have performed twice with the Nashville Symphony as part of a side-by side program.
