Lloyd Waller is a bassoonist currently studying instrumental performance at the University of Miami located in Coral Gables, Florida. He is currently in his sophomore year. As student of the Frost School of Music, Lloyd Waller frequently performs with the Frost Wind Ensemble in addition to various other chamber ensembles. Lloyd Waller has been studying the bassoon since 2011, and has studied with prolific bassoonists such as the late Hunter Thomas, former Principle Bassoon of the Huntsville Symphony Orchestra, Julia Harguindey, current Principle Bassoon of the Nashville Symphony Orchestra, and Gabriel Beavers, Associate Professor of Bassoon at the University of Miami Frost School of Music. After school, Lloyd Waller intends to pursue a career in orchestral performance. Lloyd Waller is also an avid composer and enjoys writing works for small chamber ensembles.
